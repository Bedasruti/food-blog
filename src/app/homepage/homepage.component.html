Receipe Of The Month

Mr Subrata Gupta

Time Taken:1 hour.
Ingredients:

  1. 600 gm basmati rice.
  2. 4 tablespoon mint leaves.
  3. 2 teaspoon coriander powder.
  4. 1 tablespoon garlic paste.
  5. 2 tablespoon tomato puree.
  6. 400 gm white onion.
  7. 8 green cardamom.
  8. 1 tablespoon milk.

Method:Take chicken into a mixing bowl. Add whole garam masala spices, salt as required, turmeric powder, red chili powder, biryani masala, ginger garlic paste, coriander powder, fried onions, half a lemon, yogurt, mint leaves, coriander leaves, oil and mix well. Leave the marinade for an hour.
